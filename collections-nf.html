We want to help you choose an Agalite collection that best matches your personal style, bathroom design and budget. We offer 5 different collections for you to choose from, ranging from the high-end luxurious Estate Collection that features 1/2" or 3/8" heavy glass, a frameless design and solid brass hardware to the budget conscious, Vision Collection which features 3/16" glass, aluminum framed construction and polished aluminum hardware. Each of the Agalite Collections come in a variety of different shower enclosure or tub enclosure configurations. Click on links above to learn more about each of the six Agalite Collections.
